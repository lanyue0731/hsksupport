HSK Helper

HSK(Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi) is a standard test for non-native speakers. HSK vocabulary has 6 classes. This app is aimed to help you learn HSK Word level by level .

After you choose a level, you first will see the Chinese word only, you can tap show or next button to see the corresponding English and examples.

You can add the word to master table if you are quite familiar with it and do not want to see it in the sheet anymore. Also you can put it into the remember table marking it as to remember.
